Nurseries Direct

Designing An Ecommerce Site

Nurseries Direct is a wholesale box tree website. This is meant to have multiple landscaping nurseries throughout the country ready for delivery. Made for one tree nursery owner with a lot of connections.

The Idea

Having a site that would make it easy for landscapers, developers or anyone who wants to buy boxed trees in bulk. Since this would be nation wide, the site should reflect landscapes of all types.

The Design

  • Slider Images - These images are mostly for informational purposes. Showing spring & fall trees with how the buying & shipping process works.
  • Shop Products - Quick links with attractive photos ready for the users to choose from.
  • Highlights - Additional details of room & property sizes. The contrasting black background draws attenting the the detials of the property.
  • Photo Gallery - A preview of the photo gallery with 6 photos dawing the eye from one to the next. A button below this shows that there are more photos on the next page.
  • Location - An important feature to have on the landing page is a little bit of information about the surrounding town. The background photo is of Santee, CA where the house is located.
